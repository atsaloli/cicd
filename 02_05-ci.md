#### Parallel jobs

Try running this pipeline:

```yaml
job_1:
  stage: build
  script: /bin/echo This is a mock build job

job_2a:
  script: /bin/echo I am job 2a
  
job_2b:
  script: /bin/echo I am job 2b

job_2c:
  script: /bin/echo I am job 2c 
```
